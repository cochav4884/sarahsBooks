Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Book Tracker app. Your privacy is important to us. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information from users. All data about books and progress is stored locally on your device using your browser's storage.

Use of Information

Since all information is local, it is never shared or transmitted to any server or third party.

Third-Party Content

Any books uploaded by the user remain private on your device. We do not host or access uploaded content.

Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Please review this page periodically for updates.
